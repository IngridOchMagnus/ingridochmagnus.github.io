Ingrid och Magnus Brollop

 

Hi there, welcome to my webpage!

I'm Magnus, an Assistant Professor at Stockholm Business School and an affiliate researcher at the Swedish House of Finance. I have a PhD in Economics from the University of Gothenburg and industry experience in machine learning.

My research interests center around market microstructure, decentralized finance, and machine learning applied within financial economics.

On this website, I present my research, write blog posts, and share useful resources related to my areas of expertise.
